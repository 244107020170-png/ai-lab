Laboratorium for
Applied Informatics

The Applied Informatics Laboratory at Malang State Polytechnic is an innovation center focused on developing information technology–based solutions. Through collaboration, the Applied Informatics Laboratory continuously strives to deliver relevant and useful technology, strengthening Malang State Polytechnic’s position as a leading educational institution in applied informatics.

Our Vision

Establish the three pillars of university i.e. education and teaching, research and development, and community service, but the main purpose is facilitating the process of three dharma (purpose) university.

Our Mission

Information Technology Application

Applying algorithms, data processing, and distributed systems for innovative technological solutions to improve productivity and information accessibility.

Technological Innovation for Industry 4.0 Solution

Focusing on Industry 4.0 solutions through innovative automation technologies, system integration, and efficient real-time data processing.

Quad-Helix Collaboration

Maintaining the relevance of Industry 4.0 through quad-helix synergy, fostering innovation, delivering effective solutions, enhancing efficiency and competitiveness, and driving sustainable digital transformation.

Distributed Data-Based System

Integrating distributed data with blockchain, big data, and machine learning improves the security, integrity, accessibility, and reliability of information.
