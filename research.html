Research and Innovation

The main purpose of the Applied Informatics Laboratory in the Information and Technology Department is to research, innovate, and create many solutions in multiple fields and topics. This page will display the main topics that will be researched by the Applied Informatics Laboratory, as well as displaying the products that have been developed and the partners the lab has collaborated with.

Priority Research Topic
Intelligent Self-learning of Computer Programming: Web, mobile, database, Java, gamification, and scaffolding
Decentralized system based on blockchain with Ethereum platform (supply chain, crowdfunding, dan tokenization of assets)
Asset protection and digitization using Electroencephalogram (EEG)
Digital map-based reporting system for government infrastructure (roads, irrigation, etc.)
Financial support technology, decentralization with blockchain and predictive system using data analysis
Smartfarming (Indoor and Outdoor) based on smart technology and IoT
Security Information and Event Management based on Wazuh
